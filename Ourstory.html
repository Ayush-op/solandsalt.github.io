THE IDEA OF SOL&SALT BEGAN WHEN THREE MATES ONE TWO AND THREE WANTED TO BRING THEIR PASSION FOR FOOD AND MOVIES TO THE GALAXY. AFTER THEY QUIT THEIR JOBS AS SPACE PILOTS THEY SET OUT TO START THEIR FOOD BRAND AND PROVIDE AWESOME FOOD TO PEOPLE OF MARS.

bRAND ETYMOLOGY

Focussing on the concept of Interstellar food and Mars we created a unique brand name that symbolizes The durations of a Martian Day "SOL" and the most important ingredient of our food "SALT".

We created a set of emotes which represent human smile and used them as a repeating background for print on packaging and digital use. We created a set of playful stickers to be used digitally and physically. Stickers are used almost everywhere, but they are most enjoyed in the packaging. With stickers, we can make every box different and unique for any client. Each time someone orders their box will come packed with random stickers so they can look forward to the next.  ​​​​​​​

BRANDING CONCEPT

PACKING IT THE UNIQUE WAY

Our packing is the most unique in the whole plant we believe the experience of opening the box is as important as eating the food.

FROM WHERE DID YOU GET THAT CUP?

The cup you see is entirely made out of recyclable plastic made out of plant fibre.

WHICH RESTRAUNT DELIVERED THAT?

The cardboard boxes are used for home delivery done by our trusted drones. The ink used in printing these boxes is 100% Plant based so we can do our part to conserve the environment.

CAN I FANCY A LOOK NOT A SIP?

The cans are derived out of 100% waste aluminium colloected from scrapped space ships and e-waste. It is used to deliver the all pressure based drinks directly to the customers. 

FRIES ON THE FLY

You would not have seen such unique packaging concept on the planet of mars take note of the fries box which make it look more appealing for the customer.
